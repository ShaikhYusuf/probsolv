Steps to Developing a Wide Variety of Solutions

Understand the Dual Goals

  • Raising Awareness: Ensuring people know about the concert.
  • Increasing Desire to Attend: Creating excitement and making the event appealing enough for people to show up.

Brainstorming Solutions

The Logic Tree Approach

Eggplant and Tofu listed all possible ways to communicate about their concert, such as:

  • Traditional media: Newspapers, magazines, and radio.
  • Digital channels: E-mails and websites.
  • Physical outreach: Posters and message boards.
  • In-person promotions: Performing during homeroom.

Encouraging Creativity

No ideas were dismissed initially, even if they seemed impractical.

Creating Impactful Messaging

Eggplant and Tofu recognized that their communication needed to go beyond simply sharing concert details:

  • On the radio: They aired their music to entice listeners, not just announce the event.
  • Posters and message boards: Designed to stand out visually and offer compelling reasons to attend.

Key Takeaways

  • Diverse Solutions Lead to Better Outcomes: Casting a wide net of ideas ensures no potential avenue is overlooked. Even "crazy" ideas can spark innovative approaches.
  • Focus on Dual Goals: All actions were evaluated based on how well they raised awareness and increased desire to attend.
  • Iterative Refinement: After brainstorming, Eggplant and Tofu prioritized and refined their list based on feasibility and impact.

By developing a variety of creative solutions, Eggplant and Tofu set the stage for their concert's success.
