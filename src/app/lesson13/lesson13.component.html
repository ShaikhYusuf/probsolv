Fishy Goals and Solid Achievements

The Journey of John Octopus

John Octopus, a skilled poster designer for the Mushroom Lovers, has a dream: to become a Hollywood animator and movie director specializing in CGI. His immediate goal is to purchase a computer to learn CGI animation. Using a proven problem-solving framework, John sets out to achieve this milestone.

Step-by-Step Goal Achievement Process

Step 1: Set a Clear Goal

Goal: Buy a computer suitable for learning and practicing CGI animation within six months.

Step 2: Determine the Gap

Current Situation: John lacks the funds to buy a computer.

Required Computer: A mid-range workstation with sufficient processing power and a quality graphics card, estimated to cost $1,500.

Step 3: Form a Hypothesis to Close the Gap

3A: List as Many Options as Possible

  • Save Money: Allocate a portion of his monthly allowance or earnings.
  • Earn Extra Income:
    • Take freelance design projects.
    • Tutor younger students in art or design.
    • Sell customized artwork or posters.
  • Seek Sponsorship: Approach local businesses or community organizations for support.
  • Apply for Grants or Competitions: Look for opportunities offering funding for aspiring artists.
  • Leverage Used Equipment: Consider buying a second-hand computer or borrowing one temporarily.

3B: Select the Best Ideas

Combine saving money with earning extra income through freelance design and tutoring. Explore the used equipment market for cost-effective options.

Step 4: Check the Hypothesis

4A: Determine Required Analyses and Information

  • Calculate the exact amount he can save monthly.
  • Research potential freelance design platforms or local opportunities.
  • Identify reliable sources for affordable used computers.
  • Investigate tutoring rates and demand in his area.

4B: Analyze and Develop an Action Plan

Action Plan Timeline:

  • Month 1-2:
    • Start saving 30% of his monthly allowance.
    • Secure at least two freelance design projects.
    • Explore tutoring opportunities.
  • Month 3-4:
    • Intensify efforts in freelancing and tutoring.
    • Shortlist potential computers to purchase, including used options.
  • Month 5-6:
    • Evaluate total savings and earnings.
    • Choose and purchase the computer.

Outcome

By systematically working through this process, John Octopus can achieve his smaller milestone of acquiring a CGI-capable computer. This success will pave the way for his larger dream of mastering CGI and pursuing a career in Hollywood.
